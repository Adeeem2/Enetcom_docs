About the Creator

This website was created by Adem Taktek, an engineering student at ENET'Com, with the aim of making academic documents easily accessible for all students across the four branches: IDSD, GII, GT, and GEC.


It serves as a centralized archive for course materials, exams, and useful resources to help students succeed in their academic journey.


If you have documents you'd like to contribute — such as PDFs, lecture notes, or old exams — you're very welcome to participate in growing this archive.
